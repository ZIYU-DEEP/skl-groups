skl_groups.preprocessing.BagStandardizer¶

class skl_groups.preprocessing.BagStandardizer[source]¶

Standardizes each feature dimension to have zero mean and unit variance, regardless of the bag it falls into.

This is just BagPreprocesser with sklearn.preprocessing.StandardScaler.
