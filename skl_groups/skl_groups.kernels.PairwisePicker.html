skl_groups.kernels.PairwisePicker¶

class skl_groups.kernels.PairwisePicker(indices)¶

Picks a subset of the passed-in matrix. Useful for choosing one of several divergences computed by skl_groups.divergences.KNNDivergenceEstimator, for example.

Rather than PairwisePicker((0, slice(2, 3))), you can also do PairwisePicker[0, 2:3].

Parameters:

indices : tuple of integers / slice objects / etc

The indices to subset the input with.
